About Me

Gabe Reed

Reed is a Computer Science and Animation student at Brigham Young University. He originally grew up in the Bay Area, California, and has lived in China, the Republic of Congo, and France before coming to BYU. Reed currently works as an animator with BYU Media Services and an iOS programmer for the BYU Chemistry department. In his free time, Reed enjoys drawing, ultimate frisbee, and riding his bike.


Projects

Reed has contributed to the following projects:

  • Avast Ye | BYU Animation Department. Reed worked as a texture artist on the award-winning game for much of 2019.
  • iORA | BYU Chemistry Department. Reed worked as head programmer and animator for an interactive 3D organic chemistry mobile app.
  • BYU IS French Tutorials | BYU Independent Study. Reed worked with another student to develop a series of French tutorials for use by students following BYU Independent Study French courses.
